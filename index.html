About Me

Hello, my name Damian Aguilar and I created this website to make it easier to share what I have been doing with my time as a student at UTD. I have thoroughly enjoyed my education here and hope to spread the community spirit and hardworking attitude wherever I may go. Thanks to my scholarship from the Terry Foundation and the generosity of the Hobson Wildenthal honors college, I have been able to experience things that never previously crossed my mind. From symphonies to trips abroad I have truly been blessed and plan to spread these oppurtunities to others who have never had the chance. So far college has shown me how vast the world really is. I've learned so much about so many different cultures and feel like I have been missing out on so much of what life offers. Throughout the rest of my college experience I want to truly understand who I am and the what dreams I will tackle. Until then, I will live the best life I can with the people I've met along the way.
